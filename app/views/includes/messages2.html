ABOUT

If you ever posted a project to GitHub and then nothing happened to it, you'll understand the need for CodeFog.
If you happen to know a bunch of coders with spare time to work on your codebase in a meaningful way, then you don't need CodeFog.
However, if you are willing to teach eager junior developers your codebase, they are willing to evangelize and become great coders with your guidance.
